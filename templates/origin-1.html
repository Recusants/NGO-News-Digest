Clean Water Access Transforms Rural Community in Masvingo

By Tendai Moyo May 18, 2023 5 min read

A partnership between WaterAid Zimbabwe and local NGOs has brought clean water to over 5,000 households in the Masvingo province, reducing waterborne diseases by 70% and transforming the lives of rural communities.

Community Transformation

The initiative, launched in 2021, focused on drilling boreholes, constructing water kiosks, and training community water committees to manage and maintain the water points.

Health Impacts

Local health clinics report a significant decrease in cases of diarrhea, cholera, and other waterborne diseases. School attendance has improved, particularly for girls.

About NGO News Digest

Who We Are

NGO News Digest is a Zimbabwe-based news and media initiative dedicated to sharing, amplifying, and celebrating the work of non-governmental organisations operating across Zimbabwe.

Our Mission & Vision

Vision: To be Zimbabwe's leading platform for celebrating and amplifying civil society impact.

Mission: To document, share, and elevate the work of NGOs in Zimbabwe through timely coverage.

What Makes Us Unique

  • Documenting grassroots impact
  • Showing linkages between NGO programmes and national development goals
  • Serving as a repository of positive change
