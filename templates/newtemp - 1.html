About NGO News Digest

We are Zimbabwe's premier platform for civil society news, opportunities, and connections.

Our Mission

To amplify the impact of Zimbabwe's civil society by providing a centralized platform for sharing stories, opportunities, and resources that drive positive change across the nation.

Our Vision

A Zimbabwe where civil society organizations are empowered, connected, and effectively contributing to sustainable development and social justice.

Our Core Values

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships and collective action.

Transparency

We operate with openness and accountability in all we do.

Amplification

We elevate the voices and work of Zimbabwe's civil society.

Inclusion

We ensure diverse representation across all sectors and regions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a story or announcement?

Please email your submission to submissions@ngonewsdigest.co.zw with relevant details and attachments. Our editorial team reviews all submissions within 3-5 working days.

Is there a cost to post job vacancies?

We offer free job postings for registered Zimbabwean NGOs. International organizations and for-profit companies may be charged a nominal fee.

How often is the digest updated?

We update our platform daily with new content. Subscribers receive a weekly digest every Monday morning.
